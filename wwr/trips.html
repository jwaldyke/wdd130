Wyatt White Water Rafting Co.

Level I - Family oriented trip where beginners are comfortable and everyone can enjoy a low-risk fun day on the river.

Level II - Beginners are welcome for easy to maneuver rapids. Participants will gain experience paddling while taking pleasure in the beauty on the river.

Level IV - Experienced paddlers will thrive on our intense and high-adrenaline rapids. Obstacles like holes, rock and strong eddies make this run unpredicatble and exhilarting.
